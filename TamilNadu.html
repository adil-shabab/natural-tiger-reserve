Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve

Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve (KMTR) is located in the southern part of the Western Ghats in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Here's more information about it:

Location: Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve is situated in the districts of Tirunelveli and Kanyakumari in Tamil Nadu. It covers an area of approximately 895 square kilometers.

Establishment: The reserve was established in 1988 by combining the Kalakad Wildlife Sanctuary and Mundanthurai Wildlife Sanctuary. It was declared a Tiger Reserve in 1988 under India's Project Tiger initiative.

Biodiversity: Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve is known for its rich biodiversity. Apart from Bengal tigers, it is home to other wildlife species such as Indian leopards, Indian elephants, sloth bears, Indian gaurs (bison), Nilgiri tahrs, lion-tailed macaques, Malabar giant squirrels, and a variety of bird species including the endangered great Indian hornbill.

Habitat: The landscape of Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve comprises tropical and subtropical moist broadleaf forests, evergreen forests, grasslands, and water bodies. The reserve is characterized by rugged terrain, deep valleys, and several rivers and streams, including the Tambaraparani River.

Conservation Efforts: Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve is actively involved in conservation initiatives aimed at protecting its biodiversity and ensuring the survival of its flagship species, including the Bengal tiger. Conservation efforts include habitat preservation, anti-poaching measures, community involvement, and research and monitoring programs.

Tourism: The reserve offers trekking and camping experiences for visitors to explore its natural beauty and wildlife. There are several trekking trails and viewpoints within the reserve that provide opportunities to observe animals and enjoy the scenic landscapes. Kalakad Mundanthurai is also a popular destination for birdwatching and nature photography.

Accessibility: Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve is accessible from major cities in Tamil Nadu such as Tirunelveli and Kanyakumari. The nearest airport is the Trivandrum International Airport in Kerala, while the nearest railway stations are in Tirunelveli and Kanyakumari.

Overall, Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve is an important conservation area in South India, known for its biodiversity, scenic landscapes, and conservation efforts aimed at protecting its natural heritage, including the majestic Bengal tiger.
